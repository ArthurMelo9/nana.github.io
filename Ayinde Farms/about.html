About Us

we are basically a poultry farm with a 100,000 capacity battery cage for housing our birds. The farm is located in Sunyani on a one acre size of land at a very good location.

The battery cage sytem of keeping our birds ensures that neat and whole eggs are produced all-year round. Our birds are fed with the best of rations and this is evident in the quality of the eggs they produce Regular vaccination of our birds ensure that they are always in good health. Looking for good quality poultry and their eggs? Ayinde Farms is the place for you!
